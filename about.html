Who we are

afro beads and accesories was created and founded in 2016 by lucy kanuthu
together with her dear sisters as a result of their knack for creating
timeless and statement fashion pieces.positioned as an affordable luxury
Brand we create Timeless classics that belong in your wardrode forever
Our design inspiration are drawn from color vintage pieces hence the
Colourful, retro and Afrocentric theme that runs throughout our designs
At afro beads ,we make high quality desirable Ethical pieces
because we believe it is possible to build a responsible and sustainable Fashion
indusrty hence our productions are are Handmade by some of the most talented artisans across kenya
We empower the local artisans and communities we work with through working with patners who we know and trust
who offer fair working condition and a safe working enviroment



Here at afro beads each piece is handcrafted using the finest ethically sourced raw material
